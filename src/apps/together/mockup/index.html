Project Collaboration tool is a place wherein users can easily keep track of all their projects (can even include team assignments) and how much progress is being made in each of them. People can login to view all the information with respect to the projects they are a collaborator of.

  • Name of the app

    Project Collaboration Tool

  • What collaborative activity can people do together using your app?

    People can collaborate on the various projects that they are working keeping track of who is assigned what task and their respective deadlines.

  • What is the ideal number of concurrent users?

    Ideal number of people would be around 15-20, although it depends on how much work is being done by people accessing at the same time.

  • Is this activity for fun or for something practical?

    It is meant for practical purposes wherein people can easily be able to know the progress in the project.

  • How can a person see what everyone else is doing?

    Everything that happens within a project, can be seen by everyone else who has been added as a collaborator for that respective project.

  • How can a person see the most recent result of everyone's collaborative effort?

    Whatever new tasks or projects that are newly created will appear as thefirst card

  • What can an admin see?

    An admin can see all the users of the app and also all their projects.
